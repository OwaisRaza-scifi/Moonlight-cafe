Beef Cheese Burger 🍔🧀

Beef Cheese Burger is a juicy grilled beef patty topped with melted cheese, fresh veggies, and sauces inside a soft bun.
It’s rich, filling, and packed with smoky flavor.

How We Created It

We shaped ground beef into patties, seasoned with salt and pepper, and grilled them to juicy perfection.
Added a slice of cheese on top, then layered with lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauces inside a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato & onion
  • Mayonnaise & ketchup
  • Salt & pepper
