How We Created It

We cooked minced meat with onions, garlic, ginger, and spices until dry. Stuffed the mixture into samosa pastry sheets,
folded into triangles, and deep fried until golden brown. Served hot and crisp with chutneys.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef or chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh coriander
  • Red chili powder & garam masala
  • Salt
  • Samosa pastry sheets
  • Oil (for frying)
