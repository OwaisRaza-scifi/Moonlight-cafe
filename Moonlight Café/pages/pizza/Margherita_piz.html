Margherita Pizza is a simple yet classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese,
and fresh basil leaves. Light, cheesy, and full of authentic flavor.

How We Created It

We spread homemade tomato sauce on fresh pizza dough, topped it with mozzarella slices,
and baked until the cheese melted perfectly. Finished with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & black pepper
