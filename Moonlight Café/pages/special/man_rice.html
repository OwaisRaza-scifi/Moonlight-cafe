Manchurian with Rice is an Indo-Chinese classic ‚Äî crispy fried manchurian balls (chicken or veg) tossed in a tangy, garlicky soy-chili sauce

How We Created It

We made seasoned chicken/veg balls, deep-fried until crisp, then stir-fried garlic, spring onions and sauces on high heat to make a glossy,
slightly sweet-spicy gravy. Tossed the balls in the sauce so they stay crisp-yet-coated, and served with freshly made fried rice that soaks up the flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken mince or mixed vegetables (for veg manchurian)
  • Flour & cornflour (coating)
  • Egg (optional, for binding)
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Spring onions
  • Soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup
  • Sugar, salt, black pepper
  • Rice (for fried or steamed rice)
  • Mixed veggies for fried rice (carrot, peas, capsicum)
  • Oil for frying & stir-fry
  • Sesame oil (optional finish)
