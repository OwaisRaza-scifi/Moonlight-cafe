Bone Marrow Nihari is a rich variation of traditional nihari, cooked with beef shank and marrow bones. The marrow melts into the gravy,
giving it a deep, buttery taste and silky texture. Served best with naan for a hearty meal.

How We Created It

We slow-cooked beef shank along with marrow bones in a spiced gravy, let the marrow release into the stew,
and thickened it with wheat flour. Finished with fresh ginger, coriander, and lemon for balance.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank pieces
  • Marrow bones (nalli)
  • Onions
  • Wheat flour
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Red chili powder
  • Whole spices (cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander, ginger slices & lemon
